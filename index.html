Hey, I'm Abhi — a songwriter and producer based out of Pune. Music has been my language since I was 12, when I first picked up a guitar and realised I could say things with a chord that I couldn't say with words.

My sound sits somewhere between lo-fi R&B, indie pop, and raw acoustic energy. I write about love, loss, late nights, and everything in between — the stuff nobody talks about at the dinner table.

I've played at indie festivals, collaborated with producers across India and abroad, and I'm always working on something new. If my music finds you at the right moment — that's everything to me.
